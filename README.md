# Project Icarus 🌊
> **"The AI wrote it is never an acceptable answer. If you can’t explain your work, it’s not your work."**

Project Icarus is a **Universal Integrity & Logic Auditor**. In an AI-saturated era, Icarus acts as the necessary friction to ensure human accountability. It identifies "Wax Wings"—unverified logic—and forces the creator to earn "Feathers" through Socratic defense before the project is allowed to "fly."

---

## 🏛️ The Vision
In an age where anyone can "generate," few can "explain." Like the mythical Icarus, many fly with wings they didn't build. This project bridges the gap between AI-generated output and true human mastery. Icarus ensures that **human intelligence remains the architect**, and the AI remains the structural inspector.

## 🛠️ System Architecture (The "No-Rework" Stack)

Icarus is a **Decoupled, Universal Web Application** designed for high-concurrency logical auditing.

### **Core Stack**
* **Frontend:** [Angular](https://angular.io/) (TypeScript) | **Signals**-based reactive UI.
* **Backend:** [FastAPI](https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/) (Python) | Asynchronous logic engine and universal file parser.

* **Persistence:** [PostgreSQL](https://www.postgresql.org/) (via **Supabase**) | **pgvector** for semantic history retrieval.

## 🔍 The Audit Mechanics

### 1. Global Insight (The Nest Scan)
Icarus performs a **User-Triggered Recursive Scan** of your entire directory. It maps dependencies across files to identify how a change in a core utility might create "Melt Risks" in dependent modules. Unlike standard AI, Icarus analyzes the **Cross-File Logic**, not just individual snippets.

### 2. Dual-Track Auditing
* **Structural Integrity (The Skeleton):** Analyzes the architectural soundness. Does the work stand on its own?
* **Human Mastery (The Soul):** Probes the user’s understanding of "Complexity Hotspots" identified during the scan.

### 3. The Batch-Audit Gate (The State Machine)
Audits are conducted in **Flights**. Icarus generates a batch of **N** questions and enters a **Lockdown State**. 
* **The Gate:** No further questions or progress can be made until the user modifies the source files. 
* **The Unlock:** The **Delta Analyzer** compares the new file hash. Only when the structural flaw is resolved and the mastery defense is verified does the Gate open for the next Flight.

---

## 🪶 The Flight Metrics: Wax vs. Feathers

* **The Wax Meter (Risk Index):** Measures "Logical Entropy." High wax indicates unverified content or vague reasoning. 100% Wax = Project Meltdown (Audit Failure).
* **The Feather Count (Mastery Index):** Earned through successful defenses and structural fixes.
    * **Golden Feathers:** Awarded for identifying **Red Herrings**—intentional logical fallacies planted by Icarus to test human vigilance.
* **Defense Transcript:** Upon completion, Icarus generates a verifiable record of your Socratic Defense—proof of human mastery that standard AIs cannot replicate.
